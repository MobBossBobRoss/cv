Hi there. I am the founder and Prophet of the Ascetic Truth, Hugh Mann. Believe it or not, I was once like you. Every day I struggled under the pressure of endless stress in my life. My financial troubles held me under the water, and more than once I wanted to give up.

But just when things were at their darkest, I received a vision. A vision of a family of truly free people, their shackles cast off to me. I had persuaded them to free themselves, and in turn they had freed me. Together we worshipped God in such revelry, as if he were right there dancing with us.

With this inspiration I founded the Ascetic Truth. I taught others to let go of their smartphones, their gold jewelery, even their money. These were a toxic influence, causing their bearers to think only of them and not of the glory of God. And through the blessing of God I found that as each new member's burden lessened, so did mine. My financial worries dissolved like they were never there. Hallelujah!
